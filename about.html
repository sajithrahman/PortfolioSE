My Story

Crafting ERP Excellence
One Module at a Time

Hi, I'm Sajith Rahman A.M — a passionate Software Engineer & Tech Specialist based in India with 3+ years of professional experience transforming how businesses run through smart technology.

My journey began with an MS in Computer Science from Government Arts College, Coimbatore. Since then I've worked across HRMS, payroll, manufacturing ERP, CRM, and multi-company environments — and expanded my horizon into Oracle NetSuite, REST APIs, mobile app development, and networking.

My core specialty is the Odoo ERP ecosystem (v12 → v19) for both Community & Enterprise, plus hands-on knowledge of Oracle NetSuite for cloud ERP use cases. I'm proficient in Python, JavaScript/OWL, REST API design, React Native for mobile apps, and Linux server/networking basics.

When I'm not deep in code, I explore emerging technologies, contribute to technical discussions, and continuously upgrade my skill set to stay ahead of the curve.
